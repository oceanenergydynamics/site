Our Mission and The Problem

Ocean Energy Dynamics addresses two major pain points in the maritime industry: cost inefficiency and environmental impact. Our mission is to redefine maritime energy through sustainable solutions, harnessing wave power to create eco-friendly, cost-effective systems specifically tailored for yachts.

Cost Inefficiency: Luxury yachts face significant operating expenses, with fuel accounting for approximately 30% of total costs. For instance, a 70-meter yacht consumes over 8,300 liters of fuel for every 100 nautical miles, costing upwards of $7,500 per trip. Traditional engines, with 90% more moving parts, require extensive maintenance, further escalating costs. Renewable alternatives like solar panels and wind turbines are often impractical due to their large space requirements and high installation costs.

How It Works

1. Wave Capture

Waves enter through a one-way inlet, powering internal oscillators to start the energy generation process.

2. Energy Conversion

Oscillators drive hydraulic pistons, compressing fluid to spin turbines, converting wave motion into energy.

3. Power Output

The turbines generate electricity, which feeds the ship's systems, propulsion, and onboard energy needs.
