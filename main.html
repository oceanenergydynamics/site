About Ocean Energy Dynamics

Ocean Energy Dynamics harnesses the untapped power of ocean waves to revolutionize energy solutions for the maritime industry. By utilizing advanced technologies, we create wave-powered systems to generate renewable electricity onboard yachts and ships.

Our system uses innovative components such as oscillators, hydraulic turbines, and turbulence-reducing grids. This step-by-step process ensures efficient energy capture and transfer, enabling vessels to operate sustainably. Explore our solution below to see how each part of the system contributes to a cleaner future for maritime travel.

How It Works

Step-by-step process of our wave energy system:

  1. Entrance: Water enters through a one-way inlet equipped with a filter to block debris and marine life.
  2. Turbulence Reducing Grids: The water flows through grids that straighten and smooth chaotic wave currents.
  3. Oscillators: Heavy oscillators move with the waves to absorb energy while compressing hydraulic fluid.
  4. Hydraulic Turbine: Pressurized fluid powers a hydraulic turbine to generate electricity continuously.
  5. Propulsion: The remaining water exits through a jet stream, reducing drag and powering the vessel.
