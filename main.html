Revolutionizing Yachting with Renewable Wave Energy

About Us

Ocean Energy Dynamics is redefining the future of yachting by harnessing the ocean’s waves to create sustainable energy. Our innovation eliminates the need for fossil fuels, offering yacht owners a cleaner, more efficient, and cost-effective solution.

The Problem & Solution

The maritime industry, especially luxury yachts, faces a dual challenge: high operating costs and a significant environmental footprint. Yachts rely heavily on fossil fuels, leading to soaring expenses and harmful emissions.

The Problem

Luxury yachts spend 30% of their operational budget on fuel alone, amounting to $7,500 per 100 nautical miles. Additionally, a 70-meter yacht emits 26.4 metric tons of CO₂ over the same journey. These costs and emissions are unsustainable.

Our Solution

Ocean Energy Dynamics replaces fossil fuels with renewable wave energy, eliminating fuel dependency, cutting emissions to zero, and reducing operational costs by 30%.

How It Works

Wave Energy Capture

Water flows into one-way inlets, filtered to block debris and protect marine life.

Hydraulic Conversion

Turbulence-reducing grids and oscillators convert wave motion into hydraulic energy.

Electricity Generation

Hydraulic turbines convert energy into electricity to power onboard systems.
