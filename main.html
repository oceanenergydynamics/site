Our Mission & Vision

At Ocean Energy Dynamics, we are committed to transforming the yachting industry with renewable energy solutions. Our mission is to reduce reliance on fossil fuels by 30% and provide efficient, sustainable power for luxury yachts and maritime vessels.

With tropical inspiration and cutting-edge wave energy technology, we envision a maritime world powered by the seas themselves.

The Problem & Our Solution

Yachts and other vessels heavily rely on fossil fuels, contributing to carbon emissions and high operational costs. This affects the environment and burdens yacht owners seeking efficient alternatives.

Ocean Energy Dynamics introduces a wave energy generator that captures wave motion, converting it into renewable electricity that seamlessly integrates into existing yacht systems. This reduces costs, emissions, and dependency on traditional fuels.

Our Innovation

Our cutting-edge wave energy system works seamlessly to power yachts. Here's how:

  1. Step 1: Water Entry - Water flows through a one-way inlet, blocking debris and protecting marine life.
  2. Step 2: Turbulence Reduction - Grids smooth chaotic wave currents, creating optimized water flow.
  3. Step 3: Oscillators - Oscillators move with the waves, converting kinetic energy into hydraulic energy.
  4. Step 4: Hydraulic Turbine - Pressurized hydraulic fluid powers turbines, generating renewable electricity.
  5. Step 5: Propulsion Assistance - Remaining water exits as a jet stream, enhancing propulsion for the yacht.
